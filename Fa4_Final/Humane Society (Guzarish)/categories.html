OUR DONATION CATEGORIES

CHARITY FOOD.

For many people food has been a luxury more than mere necessity. While people enjoy home cooked hot meals there are children and women struggling for a bite of food while picking up the rag. NGO's collect all the extra leftover foods donated by generous people and feed the needy with the life's most basic requirement.

Homeless People

When the sunsets and the night draw near, a single light from the lamppost 20 meters away becomes the only source of light for so many street dwellers. A half-torn blanket, a shredded asbestos ceiling, and an earthen stove are all the properties of those people. They are deprived of the basic amenities in life. They do not have proper beds, drinking water, and even toilet.

Education

We play an important role in supporting the most vulnerable children access their Right to Education through innovative ideas such as forming student groups and child-tracking system, holding awareness workshops with parents to ensure that they send children to school.

Child Protection

Childhood is the most innocent phase of any individual. Every child deserves healthy and normal childhood. But for many children, the reality of childhood is altogether different as they are employed as labour and their childhood is disturbed.

Crowd Funding

We raise funds (funding) from a large number of donors (crowd) by mediums like internet, social media and mobile phones. Lot of social enterprises, start-ups, and even nonprofits are successfully raising funds by doing crowdfunding campaigns planned by us.

Donate Poor

We serve the poor elderly and helps them live a happy and dignified life. With no source of income, many senior citizens who are abandoned by their children have no place to go. HelpAge offers these people a place to stay and tends to their different medical needs.
